UTT Gulf Corporation is the pre-eminent source of sound business advice and consulting expertise in the middle east. With a combined experience of 80+ years in the nuances of the middle eastern industry landscape, the experts at UTT Gulf are able to provide their valuable clientele, accurate market intelligence, sound brand positioning & entry strategies, and access to local service providers to enhance their service offering.
Who We Are

UTT Gulf Corporation W.L.L. is a Management Consultancy & Business advisory services provider working with local and international entities alike. We are the regional arm of M/s UT Trade Corporation, Japan and represent various prestigious Asian and European OEMs in the GCC territory. The founding directors of UTT Gulf Corporation who form the core of our management team have a combined 80+ years of experience in Business Development and Management Consulting in the GCC.
What We Do

UTT Gulf Corporation W.L.L. was established primarily as a Business development & Management Consultancy Service Provider for providing local representation advisory to reputed international OEMs primarily from Asia & Europe. Our Service Offerings include: Business Development Business Consultancy Local Agency Support Industrial Asset Management Consultancy (Shutdown Maintenance Support, Overhaul Support, Repair Workshop Support for Rotating Equipment). Technical Advisory Owing to our rich history and association with the business landscape in Bahrain and GCC region, we are the partners of choice to assist our international principals in achieving access to upcoming projects with National and International Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power & Utilities projects. We also provide active assistance to our clients in forging functional relations with local service providers of Manufacturing, Fabrication, Manpower, Equipment, and other resources. With an experienced team of dedicated professionals, UTT Gulf has been able to script success stories for all its international partners and emerge as the preferred local partners for Bahrain & the GCC region.
Our Presence

Our location at the heart of the Gulf in the Kingdom of Bahrain makes access to every market in the Middle East quick and efficient – by road, air and sea. Saudi Arabia, the Gulf’s largest economy, is less than an hour’s drive away, while UAE, Oman and Qatar are less than an hour’s flight away. To harness merits of this unique advantage we have built a strong presence in the Gulf region, through strategic alliances with native entities in Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates and Oman.
